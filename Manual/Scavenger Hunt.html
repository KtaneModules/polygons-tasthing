Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Scavenger Hunt

On the Subject of Scavenger Hunts

You ever just be walking and all of a sudden your map gets encrypted in a made up langauge?

This module contains a 4x4 maze in which the walls cannot be seen. Your current position is indicated by a symbol on one of the tiles. Press the ridges on the edge of the module to navigate. Two positions must be submitted to disarm the module — Press the grid to submit your current position. Take the last digit of the serial number modulo 6 to identify which maze is active.
